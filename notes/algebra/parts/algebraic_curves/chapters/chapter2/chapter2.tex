\chapter{Affine Varieties}\label{chapter_2}

Throughout this chapter, we let $k$ denote a fixed algebraically
closed field. We may also assume that all rings in this chapter
(unless otherwise specified) contain $k$ as a subring, and any ring
homomorphisms are fixed by $k$; i.e. if $\phi$ is a ring
homomorphism, then $\phi(\l)=\l$ for all $\l \in k$.
